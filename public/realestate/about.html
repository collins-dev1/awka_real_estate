Our Story

The journey to building Nigeria's most trusted real estate association

The Real Estate Agents Association (REAA) was born out of a pressing need to address the rampant fraud and unprofessional conduct plaguing the real estate sector in Anambra State. For years, innocent citizens fell victim to unverified agents, fake property deals, and financial losses.

In response to these challenges, a group of dedicated real estate professionals came together with a singular vision: to create a self-regulating body that would restore trust, protect consumers, and elevate the standards of real estate practice across the state.

Today, REAA stands as the official government-recognized regulatory body for real estate agents in Anambra State. We have successfully registered and verified hundreds of agents, prevented countless fraud cases, and established a digital platform that allows anyone to verify an agent's credentials in seconds.

Our journey continues as we expand our reach across all local government areas, partner with government agencies, and build partnerships with law enforcement to ensure that only verified, professional agents operate in our state.
